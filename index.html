Reaching BITS Goa

NH 17B, Bypass Road, Zuarinagar, Sancoale, Goa 403726
BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus

From Dabolim Airport

Approx 7 kms

By Cab: Rs 300/- (Yes, Cabs are expensive here)
We sadly don’t have Ola or Uber ( Here’s why )

By Bus: Rs 20/- (Buses are relatively cheap)
You’ll take a bus from Airport to Dabolim bus stop, there you’ll have to take another bus to Zuarinagar. From Zuarinagar it’s a 1 km walk.
PS: Not recommended If you want to travel comfortably.

From Vasco Railway Station

Approx 10 kms

By Cab: Approx Rs 500/-

By Autorickshaw: Rs 200/-

By Bus: Rs 20/- (But again, not recommended for comfortable travel)

 established in 2004

 2600 students on campus

 18 hostels in all

 188 acres of campus area

Places To Visit

Bored? It's time to explore

Bogmalo

Approx 7 kms

BITSian's beach.Half of the campus can be found at this beach on saturdays and sundays. If you are a fitness enthusiast then you can jog till the beach and sit and enjoy the sunset while sipping water (because you are a fitness enthusiast :p) There are a lot of cool restaurants around this place, which includes:
John Seagull, Claudi’s corner, Joet’s

Sao Jacinto Island

Approx 6 kms

Again, joggable.
Restaurants to try: Sheela Bar & Restaurant

Heart Shaped Lake

Approx 8 kms

Dudhsagar Falls

Approx 70 kms

A place for all trekking enthusiasts.
College’s trekking club usually organises expeditions to Dudhsagar falls every year, so you might as well join them.

Fort Aguada

Approx 40 kms

Fort Aguada and its lighthouse is a well-preserved seventeenth-century Portuguese fort standing in Goa, India, on Sinquerim Beach, overlooking the Arabian Sea.

Three Kings Chapel

Approx 6 kms

It’s considered to be a haunted place. So go explore after the sun sets.

Chapora Fort

Approx 42 kms

The fort sits on a prominent position which commands views in all directions. It also has steep slopes on all sides.
This is where the famous scene fro Dil Chahta Hai was shot.

Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church

Approx 25 kms

The Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception Church is located in Panjim, Goa, India. The Church conducts mass every day in English, Konkani, and Portuguese.
This church has a bollywood connection too. Try to figure out.
